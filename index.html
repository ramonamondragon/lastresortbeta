Welcome to The Last Resort!

Meet Sophia and Ramona (and Mr. Bones), two very living human women who are hired to work at a hotel for spirits in the afterlife! A job that includes, but is not limited to:
  • Navigating The Vast, a.k.a. The Afterlife, a huge endless world with all sorts of biomes and creatures inhabiting it
  • Helping their boss, Mr. Bones, run one of many hotels for people in the afterlife. There will be many shenanigans.
  • Getting paid in exposure... TO FUN!
  • Helping shapeless cute blobs decide what they want to look like in the afterlife.
  • Dealing with and confronting their inner demons, haunting them at every turn.
  • Fulfilling quests and requests for guest all around the Vast that are fun and cool and only sometimes potentially lethal.
  • Fighting off the presence in the Afterlife out to get them, God knows why, please, they just want to do their job, they're just the employees.
  • Being haunted by the ghostly grandma chef because they didn't finish their meal.
  • And so much MORE!
